Definition of Done

An AI eval is considered done when it meets the criteria below. Use this checklist before marking an eval complete or requesting review.

AI Eval completion checklist

  • ○Eval has a clear, documented objective and success criteria
  • ○Dataset follows the standard format (see Dataset Format)
  • ○At least one metric is defined and validated against a small sample
  • ○Eval runs successfully in local dev and produces repeatable results
  • ○CI job is configured and passing (or explicitly excluded with a ticket)
  • ○README or doc comment describes how to run the eval and interpret results
  • ○No PII or sensitive data in datasets or logs
  • ○Ownership and contact are documented (e.g. Add your name to the project card on the Current Projects page)

Other things to consider

Depending on the nature of your project, you may require additional/different requirements to be considered before an eval is considered done.

  • Accessibility: ensure our AI tool meets the standards in the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG).
  • A11y minimum requirements (deck): a11y-minimum-requirements.pptx. For the written checklist, see the dev-docs markdown.
  • Create custom Cursor rules, skills and commands to help with building your eval process.
